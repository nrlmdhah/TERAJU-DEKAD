About us

Safeguarding Organizations with Expertise

TERAJU DEKAD SDN BHD (881276-D) is a private security management company established on 2 December 2009, providing a wide range of security services for institutions and individuals. The company aims to maintain a strong reputation as a reliable and trusted security and investigations provider.


We are committed to delivering high-quality security and investigative services with strong professionalism, reliability, and confidentiality. The company emphasizes client trust, tailored security solutions, and raising security awareness to support clients’ organizational needs, striving to be a dependable and reputable security partner.


Our Services

STATIC GUARD

Well trained and disciplined guards are placed at assignments for the protection of client’s property. A thorough security survey will be conducted and recommendations forwarded to clients.

GUARD DOG

Our guard dog will carry out regular and periodical visits to the client’s premises and check to ensure safety.

CENTRAL MONITORING SYSTEM

Our Central Monitoring System (CMS) monitors client alarm systems to detect fire, break-ins, and emergencies, with trained personnel responding immediately.
